    How to play

    • Arrow keys, WASD, or HJKL move/bump attack.
    • . or Space waits. No simulation advances until you act.
    • g picks up items. Echo shards are collected automatically.
    • i opens inventory. Potions and scrolls identify themselves when used.
    • > descends stairs. Escape with a true memory from floor 6.
    • Hover cells for rich tooltips. Death deletes the active save but banks echoes for future max-HP bonuses.
